A sponsored mobile game from cheetos which had three augmented reality games for the three new cheetos flavors. every game have a unique gameplay.

Indian

Cheetos peaces falling from the sky and you have to catch all of them while an indian guy playing music for the snakes. if you miss one, the indian guy will get bitten by the snake.

Thai

Shoot a cheetos peaces on the mouth of a frog on the tree after a while the frog will turn into a dragon.

Mexican

Shoot a cheetos peaces on the mouths of mexican guys in a bar. each time you feed one of them there moustaches become bigger.
