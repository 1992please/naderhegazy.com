This was my first project on unreal engine. It was a single/multi player VR game (arcade style) where players shoot zombies and try to survive for at least five minutes. It was really a cool journey learning Unreal Engine and doing this project at the same time.

Zombies AI.

Zombies AI is pretty simple. They just move to the closest player and start hitting him until he dies or they die. They have different running speeds: sneaking, walking and running. Every zombie has a different attack speed, damage, and moving speed.

Spawning Zombies.

Spawning cubes are added all over the maps and zombies are spawned randomly in a random location inside the cubes.
